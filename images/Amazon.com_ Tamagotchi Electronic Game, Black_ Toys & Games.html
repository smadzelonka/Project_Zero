5.0 out of 5 stars Great return of the 90's classic!
Reviewed in the United States on September 30, 2018
Color: BlackStyle: Electronic GameVerified Purchase
I was pretty excited when the Tamagotchi Mini came out, but it was too basic for my tastes. So I was even more excited to see they had done well enough to bring back the full original Tamagotchi devices. I immediately pre-ordered this black one, and it arrived today (9/30/18).

I definitely recommend it. I'm sure nostalgia is a factor, but it's fun to have this little device back in stores. Even without reading the instructions it's easy enough to figure out the controls, so if you used one in the past you should be fine otherwise read the instructions. Keep in mind that while it's very demanding at first with its hunger/happiness meters draining quickly, that's to represent it being a baby and once it starts to age a little you don't need to babysit it as much.

10/19/18 edit: My first guy made it to 18 years before dying and now I'm a few days into my second. I still really enjoy it. I will admit there are a couple quality issues with design - namely sound and the screen. Sometimes the internal speaker kind of "squeals," but it's not constant for me and sometimes applying pressure can get it to stop.

Also the screen isn't perfect and can be a little blurry if looked at straight on, at an angle is when it looks best, and I've read online that tightening some internal screws can fix this issue but I haven't tried to do that yet since it's not that big of a deal. Just thought these points were worth coming back to mention. Still a lot of fun and I still 100% recommend it! I'm really hoping we get more Tamagotchi or maybe even Digimon here in the West.
